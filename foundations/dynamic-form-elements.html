Form behaviour

Business.gov.au dynamic forms use progressive disclosure to display form fields one at a time. Users are presented with one initial question. When they have answered that question the next one appears. The submit button appears when all the form fields have been filled in.

Error states are provided for form fields below. These will not always be needed as the progressive disclosure method minimises user error. It is not possible to progress to the next field without successfully filling in the field before it.
